About Zara Therapy Clinic

The Psychology and Therapy Clinic provides psychological therapy and care in an ethical and professional manner. The COVID period has been a trying time for many, with COVID restrictions and lockdowns having significant impact the mental health of people in our community and our way of life. Our Clinic offers psychological sessions that provide high Medicare rebates for clients on a GP Mental Health Plan (with small gap fees). We can also see clients via Private Health. We are still offering support to clients in a safe practice and we offer Telehealth sessions (having video sessions via a secure platform). We also have limited Face to Face sessions again.

“the Zara Therapy Clinic” is an occupational therapy practice which was established in VietNam in 2000. The focus is on goal directed intervention to ensure optimal functioning of both children and adults. A strong emphasis is placed on the quality of intervention and thorough approach to implementation. This is done within the framework of insightful assessments, clinical interpretation of findings within the specific context of each individual and specific goal-directed therapy. For child intervention, regular consultation and liason with the parents/caregivers is engaged in, to ensure carry over of management into the home and school environment and hence maximum progress. With adults, much support and mentoring is effected to obtain optimal functioning of the individual in daily life.
